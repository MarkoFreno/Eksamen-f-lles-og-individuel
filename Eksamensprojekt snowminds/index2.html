KOM UD TIL REFFEN

REFFEN DENGANG

Refshaleøen ligger som en ø i Københavns havn. Den var hjemplads for Danmarks største arbejdsplads Burmeister & Wain, som havde ikke mindre end 10.000 medarbejder under sig, som alle arbejdede ude på Refshaleøen. De producerede motorer, kedler og centrifuger.

Inden dette kæmpe firma overtog pladsen på Reffen, var der ikke andet end sandbanker derude. Københavns havnevæsen besluttede sig for at flade arealet ud, og derved skabe nyt land.

REFFEN I DAG

Efter at det store firma gik konkurs i 1996, blev øen overtaget af Refshaleøens Ejendomsselskab, som til den dag i dag, udlejer lokaler på øen til alle slags mennesker. I dag ligger der bl.a. arbejdspladser, gallerier, strandbar, street food marked, yacht hotel og meget meget mere.

Øen er også kendt for sine mange events der forgår derude. Her er Distortion, Copenhell bare nogle af de store events der bliver afholdt på øen.

Øen er et samlingspunkt for rigtig mange forskellige mennesker, turister valfarter derud for at spise den gode mad og drikke de gode øl. Men også for at tilbringe sine timer på de gode spots der findes rundt omkring på øen.

KOM MED EN TUR PÅ REFFEN!

Toll Havs

Toll Havs is a diverse and lovely café, which opens on Refshalevej 151 in the summer of 2021. On the café you can get a large assortment of delicious snacks, as well as good drinks. There are also nice items for the sweet tooth on Toll Havs. Moreover, it is also a nice and cheap alternative to the rest of the food places on Reffen. So, swing by to see the enchanting assortment on Toll Havs. Who knows, maybe even for a dip in the Habour-pool right beside us. See you there?

Amass

Amass is a delicious gourmet restaurant with lots of nice opportunities for nice food experience. Beside of good food for meat lovers, they also offer nice menus for vegetarians and vegans. Furthermore, Amass has a series of blasting wines, which of course are arranged to the specific dish by a sommelier. In that way its possible to reach a unique combination of good food and nice beverages.

Mikeller

Mikkeller is close to the tip of Refshaleøen. Mikeller is a microbrewery which is owned by Danes. It was founded in Copenhagen in 2006 and today consists of more than 50 places around the country. If you want a special beer and a nice experience, you might want to give this a shot.

La Banchina

La Banchina is a restaurant that consists of 16 seats, which are often occupied. La Banchina is just in front of the habour-pool, which gives it a great location. It is a calm place to relax and enjoy some vegetarian food, as well as seafood.

Copenhill

Copenhill is an urban mountain spot. It offers a lot of nice skiing as well as good company, and a nice environment to meet new friends. If you want to experience something adventurous on Refshaleøen, then this is the place to go.

Alchemist

Alchemist is hard to explain with words really. Alchemist is a restaurant with dynamic food, in a unique and special way. On Alchemist its possible to buy more than 50 different dishes, and of course beverage that fits right to it.

The Habour-pool

The Copenhagen habour-pool is very attractive during the busy summer months. It is a lovely place right next to Urban Rigger, where you can always get a nice dip and cool down. Its close to both Toll Havs and La Banchina, so if you want to grab a beer in the warm sun, its right next to Toll Havs’ backyard.

Lille Bakery

Lille Bakery is a mix of both the Danish and English words. Lille means little. This one sticks out a bit, as it is the only bakery on Refshaleøen. Here you can get both nice sourdough bread, as well as some unique rye bread. It is also possible for you to find some fresh drinks, like milk, juice, or smoothies.

INFO OM CAFFÉEN

HUSETS HISTORIE

Denne gule bygning som i dag indrammer Snowminds kontorbygning, har en lang historie bag sig og dens placering er ikke tilfældig.

Denne gule bygning blev i 1800 tallet brugt som told hus, her skulle skibsmænd og fragtskibe betale deres told når de enten kom ind i landet eller skulle forlade det. Derfor er dens placering også noget af det første du ser når du kommer ind på Refshaleøen. Grunden til navnet på Cafeen kommer også fra denne historie.

IDÉ OG KONCEPT

Den samme gule bygning som krævede penge fra de sejlende folk, skal nu bruges til at oprette øens kiosk/cafe. Caféen Toll Havs skal invitere til hygge på de store græsarealer, samt den store terrasse.

Her vil der blive mulighed for at sætte sig med en øl, kaffe, te, vin osv. Der vil blive plads til alle slags mennesker, turister som lokale, der er priser som rammer den helt normale brugere, men også specialvare som skal tiltrække vinelskeren samt specialøl til øl eksperten.

Maden der bliver serveret her, vil blive i en grab ’n go stil, hvor du træder ind cafeen, henter dig noget at spise i form af special brød, toast lavet med lækre råvarer, og sætter dig ud på vores lækre terrasse eller de nærliggende grønne områder.

Om os

Velkommen til vores nyopstartede café på reffen. Vi vil skabe en hyggeligt atmosfære for alle der kommer hos os. Velkommen til Toll Havs.
